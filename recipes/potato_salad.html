Potato Salad

This easy potato salad recipe with old-fashioned flavor is quick to make. It will become a go-to summer side dish. Best made the day before.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds red potatoes, chopped
  • 2 cups finely chopped pickles
  • 3 cups mayonnaise
  • 5 large hard-cooked eggs, chopped
  • ½ cup chopped red onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 3 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
